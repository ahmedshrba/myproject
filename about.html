    The College of Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Al-Qadisiyah was established on May 26, 2008, in the Departments of Mathematics and Computer Science. Its creation was one of the fruits of a specialized scientific symposium to research ways and requirements for advancing the reality of mathematics, which was held at the Faculty of Science - University of Qadisiyah for the period December 20-21, 2006. The day we were assigned the duties of the dean of the college. Since its establishment, the college has been consolidating the educational environment that will achieve efficient performance and quality in higher education and scientific research, using advanced techniques that ensure the desired advancement in the efficiency of the faculty, which will inevitably reflect on the level of college graduates.
